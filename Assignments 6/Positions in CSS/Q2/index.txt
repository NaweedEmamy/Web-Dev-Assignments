Difference between Absolute and Relative positioning:

Absolute Position:
In Absolute positioning, the item is positioned relative to its first non-static parent. The item is removed from he current area and is treated as a different layer.
The removed item do not leave its space behind.
Relative Position:
In Relative positioning, the item is positioned relative to its current position. It is still treated in the same layer.
The positioned item will leave its space behind.
